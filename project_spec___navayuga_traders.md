# Navayuga Traders - Website Project Specification

## Overview
Navayuga Traders is a premium agricultural trading company bridging the gap between farmers and wholesalers/retailers.

## Pages to Build
1. **Home**: Hero, Stats, Why Choose Us, Featured Products, Process, Testimonials.
2. **About Us**: Mission, Vision, Journey, Team, Supply Chain.
3. **Products**: Categorized grid (Grains, Pulses, Seeds, etc.) with search and filters.
4. **For Farmers**: "Become a Supplier" benefits and registration form.
5. **For Buyers**: Bulk supply benefits and "Request Quote" form.
6. **Services**: Detailed breakdown of Procurement, Warehousing, Logistics, etc.
7. **Gallery**: Visual showcase of operations and partnerships.
8. **FAQ**: Structured questions for farmers and buyers.
9. **Contact**: Inquiry form, map, and direct communication links.

## Key Features
- Responsive Mobile-first design.
- Sticky glassmorphism navigation.
- Interactive process timeline.
- Premium animations and hover effects.
- Multi-step forms for suppliers/buyers.
- Floating WhatsApp and Call buttons.
